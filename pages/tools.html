Engineering Calculators

Every calculator runs in your browser, no signup, no data collected. Each cites its source code (ASME B31.3, ISO 5167, API RP 14E…) and includes a brownfield reality-check. Use them as a sanity check, not as a substitute for stamped engineering.

Honest Assessments · Field Perspective

Software Reviews

The design, scanning and analysis tools I actually use on real projects. Honest assessments from someone who’s on the model every day — not marketing copy, not vendor pitches.

3D Piping Design

AVEVA E3D / PDMS
AVEVA
The industry standard for large EPC projects. Steep learning curve, powerful spec-driven workflow. Dominant in European and Middle Eastern petrochemical.
Industry standard for major EPCs
AutoCAD Plant 3D
Autodesk
More accessible entry point, widely used by smaller engineering firms. Good integration with AutoCAD ecosystem. Catalog-driven. Familiar if you come from a 2D AutoCAD background.
Strong for mid-size projects
SmartPlant 3D / S3D
Hexagon / Intergraph
Rule-driven design with strong integration to SmartPlant suite. Common on large US and Asian projects. Data-centric approach. Heavy infrastructure investment required.
Large project / US market focus
Navisworks
Autodesk
The go-to for model review, clash detection and 4D scheduling. Not a design tool — an aggregation and review tool. Essential for multi-discipline coordination.
Essential for clash review

Scan-to-Model & Point Cloud

Leica Cyclone
Leica Geosystems
Industry-leading point cloud registration and processing. The benchmark for accuracy. Pairs naturally with Leica RTC360 and BLK360 scanners.
Benchmark for accuracy
Autodesk ReCap
Autodesk
Point cloud management and preparation. Integrates directly with Plant 3D and Navisworks. Good for importing and cleaning scans before design work.
Seamless Autodesk integration
CloudCompare
Open source
Free, powerful, and surprisingly capable for point cloud analysis, comparison, and basic processing. Steep learning curve but excellent for verifying registration quality and change detection.
Free — no excuse not to learn it
Trimble RealWorks
Trimble
Strong alternative to Cyclone for registration and feature extraction. Paired with Trimble X7 scanners. Growing presence in construction and industrial markets.
Strong Trimble ecosystem fit

Stress Analysis

CAESAR II
Hexagon
The global standard for pipe stress analysis. Every stress engineer knows it. As a piping designer, you don’t run it yourself — but you need to understand what it’s checking and how your routing decisions affect the results.
Global stress analysis standard
ROHR2
SIGMA Ingenieurgesellschaft
Widely used in European (especially German) engineering. Full-featured stress analysis with strong code coverage including EN 13480. Common on German and Dutch EPC projects.
European market focus
